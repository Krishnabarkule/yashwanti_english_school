About Yashwanti English School

Excellence in Education Since 1985

Our History

Founded in 1985 by visionary educator Mrs. Eleanor Sunshine, Yashwanti English School began as a modest institution with just 50 students and 5 teachers. What started in a small building has now grown into a sprawling campus serving over 1,500 students.

Over the past 35 years, we have consistently maintained our commitment to academic excellence while adapting to the changing educational landscape. Our journey has been marked by numerous milestones, including recognition as one of the top schools in the region and accreditation by national educational boards.

Today, we stand as a testament to the power of quality education, having produced thousands of successful alumni who are making their mark in various fields across the globe.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens who contribute positively to society and embrace lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, supportive, and stimulating learning environment that promotes academic excellence, critical thinking, creativity, and character development while fostering respect for diversity and individual potential.

Our Infrastructure

State-of-the-art facilities for comprehensive development

Smart Classrooms

All classrooms are equipped with interactive smart boards, projectors, and high-speed internet connectivity for an enhanced learning experience.

Library & Resource Center

Our library houses over 10,000 books, digital resources, and dedicated reading areas to foster a love for learning.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs with modern equipment for hands-on experiments and research.

Computer Labs

Multiple computer labs with latest hardware and software to develop digital literacy and coding skills.

Sports Complex

Extensive sports facilities including basketball courts, football field, swimming pool, and indoor sports arena.

Auditorium & Arts

A 500-seat auditorium with professional sound and lighting systems, plus dedicated spaces for music, dance, and drama.
