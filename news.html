Latest News

Stay updated with recent happenings at our school

Achievement

December 10, 2025

Students Win National Science Olympiad

Our Grade 10 students, led by Priya Mehta and Rahul Singh, secured first place in the National Science Olympiad, competing against over 200 schools from across the country. Their innovative project on renewable energy solutions impressed the judges and won them the gold medal.

Event

December 5, 2025

Annual Sports Day Celebration

The 35th Annual Sports Day was celebrated with great enthusiasm. Over 1000 students participated in various athletic events, showcasing their sporting talents. The event concluded with a spectacular closing ceremony featuring cultural performances.

Achievement

November 28, 2025

100% Results in Board Exams

Yashwanti English School achieved a remarkable 100% pass rate in the recent board examinations. 45% of students scored above 90%, with 12 students achieving perfect scores of 100 in various subjects. Congratulations to all students and teachers!

Event

November 15, 2025

Inter-School Cultural Festival

Our school hosted the Regional Inter-School Cultural Festival with participation from 25 schools. Students showcased their talents in dance, music, drama, and art. The three-day event was a grand success, promoting cultural exchange and creativity.

Event

November 1, 2025

Reading Month Campaign Launch

November was declared as Reading Month with various activities including book fairs, author visits, and storytelling sessions. The campaign successfully encouraged students to develop a reading habit, with over 3000 books borrowed from the library.

Community Service

October 20, 2025

Community Outreach Program

Students and teachers participated in a community outreach program, teaching underprivileged children and distributing educational materials. The initiative benefited over 500 children from nearby villages, spreading the joy of learning.

Major Achievements 2025

Our students' outstanding accomplishments

International Math Competition

September 2025

Three of our students - Aisha Khan, Vikram Patel, and Sarah Chen - represented the country at the International Mathematics Olympiad in Singapore and brought home silver and bronze medals.

National Robotics Championship

August 2025

Our robotics team won first place in the National Robotics Championship. Their innovative design of an eco-friendly waste management robot was applauded by all judges.

State Swimming Championship

July 2025

Student athlete Maya Desai won 5 gold medals at the State Swimming Championship, setting new records in freestyle and butterfly events. She qualified for nationals.

National Debate Competition

June 2025

Our debate team of Arjun Sharma and Meera Iyer won the National Debate Competition, demonstrating exceptional oratory skills and critical thinking on global issues.

International Art Competition

May 2025

Riya Gupta's artwork was selected for the International Children's Art Exhibition in Paris, representing our country among 50 nations. Her painting on climate change received widespread appreciation.

State Music Competition

April 2025

Our school choir won first prize at the State Level Music Competition. Additionally, student Kavya Nair won Best Solo Vocalist award for her classical performance.
